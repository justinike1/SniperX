The 5 real systems

1. Market scanner
This watches:
	•	price momentum
	•	volume spikes
	•	liquidity
	•	wallet activity
	•	volatility

Its only job is to find possible opportunities.

2. Decision engine
This is the “brain.”
It scores trades based on multiple factors, like:
	•	trend strength
	•	volume confirmation
	•	spread/slippage
	•	market regime
	•	risk level
	•	token safety

Then it gives each setup a score, like:
	•	85/100 = take trade
	•	60/100 = watch only
	•	40/100 = reject

3. Risk engine
This is the most important part.
It decides:
	•	max size per trade
	•	max daily loss
	•	stop loss
	•	trailing stop
	•	when to shut down for the day

Without this, the bot is not genius. It is just dangerous.

4. Execution engine
This handles:
	•	wallet balances
	•	Jupiter quotes
	•	route selection
	•	transaction sending
	•	failed trade retry logic
	•	confirmation logging

This is where most bots break.

5. Learning/reporting layer
After every trade it should log:
	•	why it entered
	•	why it exited
	•	PnL
	•	win/loss
	•	market conditions
	•	what worked and what failed

That’s how it becomes smarter over time.
